# Scan4Call Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** January 29, 2026

Scan4Call (“we”, “our”, or “the App”) values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our mobile application. By using Scan4Call, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

When you use Scan4Call, we may collect the following types of information:

* **QR Code Scanning:** When you scan a QR code, we may record the scan activity to enable calling or messaging features.
* **In-App Calls & Chats:** Information related to calls and messages made within the app.
* **Purchasing Information:** Data related to any in-app purchases you make.
* **Personal Information:** Including your full name, email address, phone number, emergency contact number, and addresses.
* **Device Information:** Your device ID and other technical information required to provide our services.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

* Provide and improve Scan4Call services.
* Enable QR code scanning and emergency calling features.
* Facilitate in-app chat and communication.
* Process in-app purchases securely.
* Contact you in case of emergency or service updates.
* Personalize your experience and improve our app functionality.

---

## 3. Sharing Your Information

We do **not** sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We may share information in the following cases:

* With service providers who help us operate the app (e.g., cloud services, payment processors).
* When required by law or to protect our legal rights.
* In case of an emergency, your emergency contact information may be used to provide assistance.

---

## 4. Security of Your Information

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration. However, no method of data transmission over the internet can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

---

## 5. Your Rights

* You can access, correct, or delete your personal information within the app.
* You may opt out of non-essential communications.
* You can disable certain app features, but this may limit functionality (e.g., emergency calls).

---

## 6. Children’s Privacy

Scan4Call is **not intended for children under the age of 13**. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

---

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted in the app with the updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
